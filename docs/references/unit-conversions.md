Quick reference for common unit conversions. Memorize the ones marked with ★ — you'll use them constantly.

## Length

| From | To | Multiply By |
|------|----|------------|
| ★ inches | centimeters | 2.54 |
| ★ feet | meters | 0.3048 |
| yards | meters | 0.9144 |
| ★ miles | kilometers | 1.609 |
| centimeters | inches | 0.3937 |
| meters | feet | 3.281 |
| kilometers | miles | 0.6214 |

**Quick mental math:**
- 1 inch ≈ 2.5 cm
- 1 foot ≈ 30 cm
- 1 meter ≈ 3 feet 3 inches
- 1 mile ≈ 1.6 km (multiply km by 0.6 for miles)
- 1 yard ≈ 1 meter (close enough for field work)

## Weight / Mass

| From | To | Multiply By |
|------|----|------------|
| ★ ounces (oz) | grams (g) | 28.35 |
| ★ pounds (lbs) | kilograms (kg) | 0.4536 |
| tons (short, US) | metric tonnes | 0.9072 |
| grams | ounces | 0.03527 |
| ★ kilograms | pounds | 2.205 |
| metric tonnes | tons (short) | 1.102 |

**Quick mental math:**
- 1 oz ≈ 28 g
- 1 lb ≈ 0.45 kg (or roughly divide lbs by 2 and subtract 10%)
- 1 kg ≈ 2.2 lbs (or roughly multiply by 2 and add 10%)
- 100 lbs ≈ 45 kg

**Quick mental math:**
- 1 tsp ≈ 5 ml

- 1 gallon ≈ 3.8 liters (roughly 4 liters)

**Kitchen volume:**
- 3 tsp = 1 tbsp
- 2 tbsp = 1 fl oz
- 8 fl oz = 1 cup
- 2 cups = 1 pint
- 2 pints = 1 quart
- 4 quarts = 1 gallon

## Temperature

★ **Fahrenheit to Celsius:** (°F − 32) × 5/9 = °C

★ **Celsius to Fahrenheit:** (°C × 9/5) + 32 = °F

**Quick mental math:** Subtract 30, divide by 2 (gives approximate °C from °F). For °C to °F: double it and add 30.

| °F | °C | Reference |
|----|-----|-----------|
| -40 | -40 | They're equal here |
| 0 | -18 | Very cold winter |
| 32 | 0 | Water freezes |
| 50 | 10 | Cool, jacket weather |
| 68 | 20 | Room temperature |
| 98.6 | 37 | Normal body temperature |
| 100 | 38 | Fever threshold |
| 140 | 60 | Hot water — burns in seconds |
| 165 | 74 | Poultry safe internal temp |
| 212 | 100 | Water boils (sea level) |
| 350 | 177 | Standard baking temp |
| 451 | 233 | Paper ignites |

## Electrical

| Term | Symbol | Unit |
|------|--------|------|
| Voltage | V | Volts |
| Current | I | Amps |
| Resistance | R | Ohms |
| Power | P | Watts |

★ **Ohm's Law:** V = I × R (Voltage = Current × Resistance)

★ **Power:** P = V × I (Watts = Volts × Amps)

**Common voltages:**
- AA/AAA/C/D battery: 1.5V
- Car battery: 12V
- USB: 5V
- US household: 120V
- European household: 230V
- US dryer/stove: 240V

## Useful Equivalents

- 1 gallon of water weighs 8.34 lbs (3.78 kg)
- 1 cubic foot of water = 7.48 gallons = 62.4 lbs
- 1 inch of rain on 1,000 sq ft of roof = 623 gallons
- 1 cord of firewood = 4 ft × 4 ft × 8 ft = 128 cubic feet
- Walking speed (average) ≈ 3 mph ≈ 5 km/h
- Sound travels ≈ 1,100 ft/sec (count seconds between lightning and thunder, divide by 5 for miles)
