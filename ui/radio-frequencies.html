FRS — Family Radio Service No License

Low-power handheld radios. Range: 0.5–2 miles typical. Channels 1–7 and 15–22 share frequencies with GMRS at 2W max. Channels 8–14 are FRS-only at 0.5W.

ChFrequencyPowerNotes

GMRS — General Mobile Radio Service License (No Exam)

Same channels as FRS at higher power (up to 50W mobile). License covers your immediate family. Repeater capable for 20+ mile range. $35 FCC license, no exam, 10-year term.

CB — Citizens Band No License

27 MHz AM radio. 4W max. Range: 2–10 miles. Widely available, no license needed.

MURS — Multi-Use Radio Service No License

VHF frequencies. 2W max. Range: 1–5 miles. Less crowded than FRS because fewer people use it.
